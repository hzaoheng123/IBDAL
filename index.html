Research areas

Trustworthy foundation models

Calibration, interpretability, and data-centric evaluations for large multimodal biomedical models.

Computational biomarkers

Learning robust biomarkers from imaging, genomics, and longitudinal records to support precision medicine.

Efficient clinical AI

Algorithmic efficiency, privacy-preserving learning, and deployment-ready systems for hospital workflows.
